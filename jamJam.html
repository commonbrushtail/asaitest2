CURATED LOCAL EXPERIENCES

JARRETT & PAOLO

Meet the dynamic dining duo: Paolo Vitaletti and Jarrett Wrisley are the brains behind Appia, Peppina and Soul Food Mahanakorn restaurants in Bangkok and now they’re bringing a new, authentic culinary experience to ASAI Chinatown.

The seasonal farm-driven menu is curated in collaboration with Paolo and Jarrett, who’ve designed comforting dishes influenced by Asian and international flavours, as well as local bites and all-day Vietnamese tapas, starring fresh ingredients from the on-site organic garden. Pair your tasty treats with a craft beer or creative cocktail, or grab a coffee, tea or juice.
