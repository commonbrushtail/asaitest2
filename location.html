ASAI Bangkok Chinatown is a lifestyle hotel located right in the heart of one of the best Chinatowns in the world.

Just steps from our door, you’ll find markets, world famous street food, hidden bars, art, history and culture. In moments, you’re on Yaowarat Road, the main artery of Bangkok’s Chinatown, abuzz with authentic sights, sounds and smells to explore, and within easy reach of the rest of the city.
